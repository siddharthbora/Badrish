About Us

Weaving Excellence from the Heart of India

"Blending tradition and technology to deliver quality, innovation, and trust in every thread."

Badrish Textiles is a family-owned leader in premium woven fabrics. With over 25 years of experience, we serve global markets with a customer-first approach and a passion for excellence. Our journey is built on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and a commitment to sustainable growth.

Our Vision: To be India's most trusted name in woven fabrics, delivering value and quality to every client, every time.

Our Journey

Foundation

Started with handlooms, building a legacy of craftsmanship and deep fabric expertise in Ichalkaranji.

1992
First Expansion

Introduced rapier machines, enabling efficient greige fabric production and scaling up for the domestic market.

2001
Technology Leap

Adopted ITEMA Cam Airjet machines from Italy, entering the garmenting fabric segment and offering dyed/finished woven fabrics.

2010
Major Scale-Up

Expanded to 30 ITEMA looms, strengthening production capacity and ensuring consistency across every meter of fabric.

2019
Next-Gen Weaving

Acquired a new unit with 25 Toyota JAT910 E-Shedding machines, enabling complex dobby designs and boosting annual capacity to 1M+ meters.

2024
The Future

Driving innovation with sustainable yarns, digital process control, and a vision to lead India's eco-conscious textile movement.

Ichalkaranji: The Manchester of India

A Textile Powerhouse

Ichalkaranji is India's textile heartbeat, home to 1,25,000+ looms and a thriving ecosystem of skilled artisans, advanced machinery, and world-class processing units. Our location ensures access to the best technology, talent, and supply chain efficiency.

  • Massive loom network: shuttle, Airjet, Rapier, Jacquard
  • Strong dyeing and finishing ecosystem
  • Product versatility: shirts, trousers, uniforms, export-quality
  • Generations of weaving expertise
  • Strategic location near Mumbai & Pune
